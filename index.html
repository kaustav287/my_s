The Nissan GT-R specifications were already revealed during the world premiere in March. It is powered by the award-winning 3.8 L V6 24-valve twin-turbocharged engine. Each unit of the aforementioned engine has been handcrafted by the TAKUMI technicians. It develops a top power of 565 bhp at 6800 rpm and peak torque of 637 Nm. It is coupled with a thoroughly-refined 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, while the engine noise is also upgraded and it has never sounded better. The GT-R features two of the most advanced All-Wheel Drive and Vehicle Dynamic Control (VDC) systems, which transmit the power evenly to the front and rear axles. It does the 0-100 kmph sprint in less than 3 seconds, while having a top speed of 315 kmph.
